I'm a new client. How do I register with THE INDUSTRY SOURCE?

Follow these 3 steps:

  1. Open the slide menu on the left, then go to the register page, and fill out the customer registration form.
  2. Upon completion of the registration form you can shop online with the app
  3. Simply log in with email address used to registered and temporary password emailed to you immediately after completion of the registration form. Please change your password afterwards in the Profile Account Information page.
